Spotify Internship

Project Role:

I worked on the Design Core team within Content Platform.

Duration:

12 Weeks

Sector:

Music Streaming

Tools Used:

Figma, Wireframing, UX Research

TypeScript, Storybook

Brief

I can't go into so much detail about my work at Spotify, however my internship with spotify lasted three months and within this time I worked on a part of the design system. In the Design system, I worked on a component, researching and designing it. Because I also have some development experience, I decided to delve into the front-end to develop the component I designed in code. I haven't used TypeScript before so that took a short while to get around, but I got my hands around it and also tried the several variations in Storybook.

My time in Spotify was made amazingly possible with the help of my Manager Lena and my amazing team #UXCP.

I also documented my period at Spotify in two articles, one which is private within Spotify and the other public and can be read below.

Takeaway

My Internship here at Spotify is one of my best working experiences and asides growing in visual design, I would say I got better in these:

  • Asking questions.
  • Understanding how feedback works and how to ask them.
  • Working in a team.
  • Collaborating with different people.
  • Planning and documentation.
